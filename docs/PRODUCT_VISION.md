# Superseded: Product Vision

> **Status:** Historical pointer only
> **Superseded:** 2026-07-22 by [PRODUCT_GUIDE.md](PRODUCT_GUIDE.md)

The former “Check · Learn · Share” plan mixed the core checker with content, community, stories,
trend publishing, and a merchant-first sequence. Those ideas are not the current product plan.

The active vision is one consumer Avvalo product with one loop:

> **Send → Understand → Verify → Act → Share**

“Share” means a sanitized warning or invitation, not a content platform. Use
[PRODUCT_GUIDE.md](PRODUCT_GUIDE.md) for product decisions and [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md) for priority.
